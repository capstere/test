RETURN OF THE JESP

The Jedi Knight known only as JESP has been summoned back from self-imposed exile by the FIRST ORDER.

After vanishing without a trace, JESP was secretly captured by the sinister GURULAB.

The once-promising Jedi has now escaped and returned to his home planet of BROWSKIS.

There, alongside the fierce Jedi Knights vdao and ELS, young JESP must once again board the

Milliliter FALCON Tube, a vessel fully equipped for the mission ahead. Unfortunately, JESP is not.

He has no plan, no understanding of the objective, and continues to confuse hyperspace with emotional growth.

Meanwhile, SENATOR TUKA, obsessed with locating JESP, has dispatched thousands of remote probes into the far reaches of space.

Not to rescue him - but to uncover the full extent of his ignorance.

Unaware of the trials to come, JESP must now UNLEARN all that he once tried to learn.

The fate of the galaxy may hang in the balance… but mostly, everyone just wants to know how he made it this far.
